## 👋 About Me

I'm a **registered nurse turned data scientist** who builds machine-learning and geospatial solutions for real-world health and public-sector problems. I'm completing an **MSc in Data Science at Addis Ababa University**, where I research within the **Resonance AI4D Lab** (College of Technology and Built Environment).

My work spans the full pipeline — from cleaning messy administrative, satellite, and clinical data to training ML models and shipping the **maps, dashboards, and decision-support tools** that make the results usable.

- 🔬 **Current research:** a multicenter, multimodal EMR model that predicts ICU **extubation failure** from time-series vitals and clinical notes (LSTM / TCN + ClinicalBERT, with SHAP/LIME interpretability).
- 🛰️ **Recent applied work:** geospatial forecasting of childhood malnutrition from national health indicators + satellite data.
- 🔌 **Building:** an IoT **health-monitoring system** on ESP32 / Arduino Uno, streaming real-time vitals through a Blynk dashboard (prototyped in Wokwi & TinkerCAD).
- 🌱 **Exploring:** explainable & fair medical-imaging AI, and big-data analytics with PySpark.
- 💬 **Ask me about:** ML for health, geospatial analytics, transfer learning, and turning data into dashboards.
